Features

Text a photo. Get a polished post.

MostlyPostly turns real salon work into scroll-stopping content that feels on-brand and on-tone for your team.

1. AI That Knows Salons

MostlyPostly understands color, balayage, blonding, lived-in tones, extensions, vivid work, men’s cuts, and more.

2. Captions, Hashtags & CTAs

Every post comes with caption, recommended hashtags (including #MostlyPostly), and a call-to-action that fits your booking flow.

3. Manager Approval Built In

Managers can review, edit, and approve posts before anything goes live — keeping your brand voice consistent.

Why it works

Built for how salons really work.

Less “We should really post more…” — more done-for-you marketing that actually gets published.

Stylists stay behind the chair

They just text a photo. MostlyPostly handles the caption, hashtags and formatting.

Managers keep final say

Every post can be routed through manager approval before it’s scheduled.

Owners see consistent posting

Regular, on-brand content that keeps your salon visible — without adding another task to your day.
