What is MostlyPostly?

MostlyPostly is an AI assistant that helps salon teams create, preview, and approve social media posts via text/chat. By providing your mobile number to MostlyPostly (e.g., during onboarding or by messaging our service number), you consent to receive automated text messages related to caption/post creation, approvals, and operational updates for your salon.
